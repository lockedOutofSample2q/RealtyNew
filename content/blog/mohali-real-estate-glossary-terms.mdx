# Mohali Real Estate Glossary: 65 Terms Every Property Buyer Must Know Before Signing Anything

In the Mohali property market, understanding technical terms like EDC (External Development Charges), RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority), and Mutation (Intkal) is non-negotiable for buyer safety. These terms define your financial liabilities, legal ownership rights, and the regulatory status of your investment. From knowing the difference between carpet area and super built-up area to identifying the importance of an Occupation Certificate (OC), mastering this vocabulary ensures you aren't overcharged or misled by developer marketing. Whether you are dealing with GMADA-allotted plots or private luxury flats, these 65 definitions serve as your primary defense against the complexities of the Punjab real estate landscape. Understanding these terms is the first step in our [Mohali real estate guide 2026](/blog/mohali-real-estate-guide-2026).

## The Complete Mohali Property A-Z

When I was on the developer side, managing project approvals across PUDA, PSPCL, and the Municipal Committee, I saw firsthand how many buyers get confused by the jargon. This glossary is designed to cut through that noise with definitions specific to the Mohali and Punjab context.

**1. Adjudicating Officer:** A judicial officer appointed under RERA to hear and decide on complaints related to compensation and interest.

**2. Agreement to Sale (ATS):** The foundational contract that outlines purchase terms before the final sale deed. It is critical for securing your rights and initiating home loans.

**3. All-in Cost:** The total amount you pay, including base price, PLCs, EDC/IDC, GST, stamp duty, and registration. Always ask for this before booking.

**4. Allotment Letter:** Issued by developers or GMADA, confirming the specific unit or plot number allotted to you after initial payments.

**5. Balcony Area:** The usable area of the balcony, now clearly distinguished from the carpet area under RERA rules.

**6. Benami Property:** Property held by one person but paid for by another, often to hide the real owner. This is illegal and carries heavy penalties.

**7. Brokerage:** The fee paid to agents (typically 1-2% in Mohali) for facilitating a deal. We believe this should only be paid for genuine advisory, not just "showing a flat."

**8. Builder:** The entity responsible for the physical construction of a building. Often works under a developer who manages the land and approvals.

**9. Built-up Area:** Includes carpet area plus the thickness of inner and outer walls, and balconies.

**10. Carpet Area:** The actual usable floor area within the walls of a unit. Under RERA, all sales must be based on this metric.

**11. Circle Rate (Collector Rate):** The minimum price set by the government for property registration. In Mohali, this varies by sector and property type.

**12. CLU (Change of Land Use):** A critical approval granted by the government to change agricultural land into residential or commercial zones.

**13. Common Area:** Shared spaces like lobbies, staircases, lifts, and parks maintained by the society.

**14. Completion Certificate (CC):** Issued by authorities certifying that the building was constructed as per the approved plans.

**15. Developer:** The entity that acquires land, gets approvals, and manages the project. Their track record is your biggest safety net.

**16. EDC (External Development Charges):** Fees collected by the developer and paid to GMADA for regional infrastructure like roads and sewerage.

**17. EWS (Economically Weaker Section):** Units reserved for lower-income groups in large projects, as per Punjab government mandates.

**18. Encumbrance Certificate:** Proof that the property is free from any legal or financial liabilities or court attachments.

**19. Escrow Account:** A separate account where 70% of buyer funds are kept to ensure they are used only for the specific project's construction.

**20. FAR (Floor Area Ratio):** The ratio of total built-up area to plot size. [FAR increases in Mohali](/blog/far-increase-mohali-2026-floor-area-ratio) have recently changed construction potential in many sectors.

**21. Floor Rise:** An additional charge for units on higher floors, common in Mohali high-rises.

**22. Freehold Property:** Property where the buyer has full ownership of both the building and the land it stands on indefinitely.

**23. FSI (Floor Space Index):** Similar to FAR, it regulates the total area that can be built on a given plot size.

**24. GMADA:** Greater Mohali Area Development Authority, the primary planning body for the region. Most [FAQ answers](/blog/mohali-real-estate-faq-2026) depend on GMADA policies.

**25. Green Zone:** Land designated for parks or forests in the master plan. Projects near these require specific Forest Department clearances.

**26. IDC (Infrastructure Development Charges):** Fees paid for major state-level infrastructure projects benefiting the locality.

**27. Intkal (Mutation):** The process of updating ownership in the revenue records after registration. This is vital for a clear title.

**28. Jamabandi (Fard):** The record of rights (ROR) of land, showing ownership, cultivation, and various rights in land.

**29. Joda Plot:** A term for two adjacent plots that can be merged into one. Popular among HNI buyers for custom luxury homes.

**30. Khasra Number:** A unique identification number assigned to a specific parcel of land in rural areas or agricultural records.

**31. Khewat Number:** An identification number for a group of owners who own a particular piece of land in the revenue records.

**32. Lease Deed:** A contract where the owner gives another party the right to use the property for a specified period.

**33. Leasehold Property:** Property where the ownership is for a fixed period (usually 99 years) rather than being perpetual.

**34. Licensed Colony:** A development that has all necessary approvals from PUDA or GMADA. This is essential for [buyer protection](/blog/licensed-plot-vs-unauthorised-colony-mohali).

**35. Lien:** A legal claim against a property used as collateral for a debt. Must be cleared before any sale.

**36. Loading Factor:** The difference between super built-up and carpet area, expressed as a percentage. Aim for 25-30% in Mohali.

**37. Loan Against Property (LAP):** A loan where you pledge your property to a bank to raise funds for personal or business needs.

**38. Maintenance:** Monthly charges for upkeep of the society. We help clients evaluate if these are [fair or excessive](/blog/mohali-luxury-project-maintenance-charges).

**39. Maintenance Corpus:** A one-time fund collected at possession to ensure long-term maintenance of the project.

**40. Municipal Committee (MC):** Local civic body that handles taxes and infrastructure in urban Mohali sectors.

**41. Mutation:** See Intkal. Essential for ensuring you are the owner in government revenue records, not just on paper.

**42. NDC (No Dues Certificate):** A certificate proving all taxes and dues are paid, required for a smooth [registration process](/blog/mohali-property-registration-process-guide-2026).

**43. NOC (No Objection Certificate):** Approvals from various departments (Fire, Forest, Pollution) needed before construction or possession.

**44. OC (Occupation Certificate):** The most important document. It proves the building is safe and legal to live in.

**45. Orange Zone:** Zoning category that allows mixed-use development under certain conditions.

**46. PLC (Preferential Location Charges):** Extra cost for corner plots, park-facing units, or specific directions.

**47. Possession Letter:** The formal invitation to take over the property after all dues are paid and OC is received.

**48. Possession Under Protest:** Taking possession while documenting unresolved issues (snags) to maintain your right to a fix.

**49. Power of Attorney (POA):** A legal document authorizing someone else to act on your behalf in property matters, common for NRIs.

**50. Prelaunch:** The stage before RERA registration or official launch. High reward but involves significant [risk considerations](/blog/prelaunch-property-mohali-risk-reward).

**51. Project:** The specific development registered under RERA, including all its phases and amenities.

**52. Promoter:** The entity that builds and sells the project, responsible for all RERA compliances.

**53. PUDA:** Punjab Urban Planning and Development Authority, the apex body overseeing GMADA and other local units.

**54. Ready Possession:** A property that has its OC and is ready for immediate move-in.

**55. Red Zone:** Industrial or specific-use zones. Essential to check before buying [industrial land](/blog/industrial-land-investment-mohali-rajpura).

**56. Registry (Sale Deed):** The final legal transfer of ownership registered at the Sub-Registrar's office.

**57. RERA:** Real Estate Regulatory Authority. Every project in Mohali must be checked on their portal for safety.

**58. Resale:** Buying a property from an existing owner rather than directly from the builder.

**59. Sale Deed:** The primary legal document of title registered with the government.

**60. SCO (Shop-cum-Office):** A commercial property type where you can run a shop on the ground floor and offices above.

**61. Servant Room:** A small utility room common in Mohali's luxury 3BHK and 4BHK apartments.

**62. Sinking Fund:** A reserve fund for major future repairs like lift replacement or structural work.

**63. Snag List:** A checklist of defects or incomplete work identified before taking final possession.

**64. Stamp Duty:** The tax paid to the state government during property registration (currently 7% total in Punjab).

**65. Super Built-up Area:** Includes carpet area, walls, balconies, and a share of common areas. Often used for marketing but not for legal pricing.
