# Sector 82A Mohali 2026: Price History, What Drove Appreciation, and What's Still Worth Buying

![Sector 82A Mohali JLPL Airport Road Commercial View](/assets/images/blog/sector-82a-airport-road.jpg)

Sector 82A in Mohali has established itself as the gold standard for high-intent HNI investment in the Tricity. As of early 2026, property prices in Sector 82A have seen a staggering appreciation, with commercial units on the JLPL Airport Road corridor moving from ₹3–4 crore a few years ago to a current trading range of ₹12–16 crore. This growth is anchored by three primary infrastructure triggers: the massive expansion of the Mohali International Airport, the maturation of the IT City corridor, and the strategic connectivity provided by the Bharatmala project. For investors, the core question is whether the entry price today still offers room for growth. The answer is yes, but only for those who look beyond the headline commercial SCOs and focus on the undervalued industrial-category plots and emerging secondary corridors within the sector that are still priced for vision-investing.
