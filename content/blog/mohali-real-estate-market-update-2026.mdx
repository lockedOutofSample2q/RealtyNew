## March 2026: Infrastructure Milestones and Rental Yield Shifts

## February 2026: The NRI Buying Wave and Stagnation Warnings

### Why did February see a spike in NRI inquiries?
Following the holiday season, many Punjabi diaspora members from Canada and the UK moved from "viewing" to "committing." However, I must warn my NRI clients: do not buy into the hype of "assured rentals." These are often just marketing gimmicks where the rental is built into your purchase price. While these areas are central and hold emotional value, they lack the "expansion capacity" that drives capital appreciation. When a sector is 100% built-out, the price can only move with inflation. ## January 2026: The New Year Shift in Buyer Psychology

### Is the Mohali property market entering a bubble?
The short answer is no, but it is becoming "selection-heavy." In January 2026, we saw the absorption of roughly 1,200 units across Mohali, but 70% of those were in projects with a proven delivery record. The market is maturing; buyers are no longer throwing money at every glossy brochure. They are checking RERA records, verifying [price history data](/blog/mohali-property-price-history-2026) on the IGRS Punjab portal, and asking about OC/CC status before signing. The financially illiterate advice of "buy anything, it will double" is finally dying out.

### The Shift Toward "Lifestyle First"
January saw a record number of inquiries from rural families in Punjab looking to relocate. These families aren't just looking for appreciation; they want proximity to Fortis, Max, and the better private schools for their children. This "end-user" demand is what provides the floor for Mohali's prices. Unlike the speculative bubbles of 2011, today's market is built on people who actually intend to live in the homes they buy.

## Quarterly FAQ: What Buyers Are Asking Right Now

### Is it better to buy a plot in a new sector or a flat in an established one?
It depends on your exit timeline. If you have a 5-year horizon, the plots in new sectors like IT City or the extended Aerocity belt offer higher appreciation potential. However, if you need immediate rental income to offset a home loan, a ready-possession flat in an established sector is the smarter financial move.

### How do I verify if a "pre-launch" is legitimate?
In Punjab, "pre-launch" without a RERA number is illegal. Always ask for the RERA registration certificate. If the developer says it's "applied for," wait until it is granted. I have seen developers face forest department approval cancellations or GMADA licensing delays that have stalled "pre-launches" for years. Never pay more than a token booking amount before the RERA number is active.

### Are circle rates in Mohali expected to rise this year?
Circle rates usually see a revision in the first or second quarter. With the recent appreciation in market values, a 5-10% hike in circle rates is likely in high-growth sectors. This will increase your registration and stamp duty costs, so if you are on the verge of a closing, it's better to finish the registry sooner rather than later.
