## 1. Title and Ownership Verification Tools
The foundation of any investment is clear title. We utilize the official **GMADA Property Search** and the **Punjab Jamabandi** portal to verify that the seller is the rightful owner and that the property is free of encumbrances. We advise our clients that a digital screenshot is never enough; always look for a certified copy with an official stamp.

## 2. Regulatory Compliance Checkers
Before we recommend any project, we run it through the **RERA Punjab registration database**. This tool allows us to see the promoter’s history, any stalled projects in their past, and the current state of their financial escrow accounts.

## 3. Financial and Yield Calculators
To determine the true ROI, we use our internal **Rental Yield Heatmap** for Mohali. This helps us compare the 3% yields in mature sectors versus the 6-8% yields seen in commercial SCOs on Airport Road.
