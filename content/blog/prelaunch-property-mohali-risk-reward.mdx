## What is a Pre-Launch Property? (Our Definition)

> **Pre-launch property investment in Mohali** is the practice of purchasing a residential or commercial unit in a real estate project before construction begins or official regulatory approvals (like RERA) are finalized. In the Tri-city region, this mechanism allows buyers to secure lower entry pricing in exchange for bearing higher development and regulatory delay risks.

## Is Pre-Launch Property Legal Under RERA Punjab?

The most critical regulatory detail every buyer must know is this: **Under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act (RERA) of Punjab, marketing, advertising, or selling a property in the pre-launch phase before obtaining an active RERA registration number is strictly illegal.**

RERA was enacted to stop developers from utilizing public funds to acquire land and finance core approvals. Legally, a builder cannot collect a single rupee of booking amount or execute any allotment agreements until they have secured all municipal approvals, environmental clearances, and an active RERA number. 

![RERA Punjab Verification Guide](/assets/images/blog/rera-sign-mohali.jpg)

To make sure you do not fall for unauthorized projects, read our step-by-step guide on [How to Verify Any Mohali Property on RERA Punjab](/guides/property-documents).

### The Under-the-Table Loophole
Despite clear legal prohibitions, developers and dealers bypass the law by framing pre-launches as:
*   "Expressions of Interest" (EOI) with fully refundable checks.
*   "Expression of Intention" bookings.
*   Private dealer allocations at highly discounted, unadvertised rates.

Builders push these pre-launches because they need quick liquidity. Securing municipal approvals from the Greater Mohali Area Development Authority (GMADA—the regional planning authority for the Tri-city) is a long process. ## The Real Risks of Pre-Launch Property Investment in Mohali

If you are a high-surplus HNWI (High Net Worth Individual) investor with liquid capital, you can afford to hold a non-yielding asset for six or seven years. But if you are a standard homebuyer looking to secure a primary residence, the pre-launch phase exposes you to three critical bottlenecks that can freeze your financial mobility:

### 1. Zero Bank Financing
Commercial banks **will not sanction home loans** on properties that do not have active building plan approvals and RERA registration. During a pre-launch, you must fund 100% of the installments using your own personal liquidity.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Block)

### Q1: Is it legal to buy a pre-launch property in Mohali?
No. Under RERA Punjab regulations, it is illegal for a developer to market, advertise, or accept bookings for any project that does not have an active RERA registration number. Pre-launch properties without RERA numbers carry immense legal and financial risk.

### Q2: Why did the cooperative housing society flats near Sector 76 Mohali stall?
Most cooperative housing society projects stalled due to internal disputes among committee members, financial mismanagement, and a lack of upfront liquid capital to fund high-rise construction, leaving retail buyers stuck without possession for years.

### Q3: What is the average price of a 2 BHK flat in Kharar?
Ready-to-move-in, RERA-approved 2 BHK apartments in Kharar currently range between **₹32 Lakh and ₹42 Lakh**, depending on the society's amenities, location, and proximity to the main highway.

### Q4: Can I get a home loan on a pre-launch property in Mohali?
No. Public and private sector banks will not sanction home loans on properties that do not have active building plan approvals and RERA registration. You must pay all pre-launch installments using your own personal liquidity.
